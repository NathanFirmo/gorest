## Commands

|Key|Action|
|---|---|
|`CTRL-H`|Go to section on the **left**|
|`CTRL-J`|Go to section **bellow**|
|`CTRL-K`|Go to section **above**|
|`CTRL-L`|Go to section on the **right**|
|`CTRL-N`|Creates a **new request** on the list|
|`CTRL-Space`|**Execute** current request|
